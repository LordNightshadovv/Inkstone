Modern Calligraphy

C
Chen Yutong
• August 16, 2025 • Slideshow Gallery
Interview with a young calligrapher exploring modern interpretations of traditional Chinese writing.

Every stroke tells a story. In my work, I blend thousand-year-old techniques with contemporary themes, creating pieces that speak to both heritage and innovation.

The theater district of Manhattan became my second home, where I discovered that emotions are universal, even when expressed in different languages and cultural contexts.

Performing Shakespeare in English while carrying the emotional depth of Chinese classical drama created a unique artistic fusion. My professors were amazed at how I brought Eastern storytelling techniques to Western classics.
