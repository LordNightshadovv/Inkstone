Street Food Chronicles

Z
Zhao Siqi
• August 11, 2025 • Slideshow Gallery
Photo series capturing the vibrant energy and cultural significance of street food across Chinese cities.

Street food is more than sustenance; it's the heartbeat of urban culture. Through my lens, I've captured the stories behind every stall, every recipe passed down through generations.

From the spicy noodles of Chengdu to the delicate dumplings of Shanghai, each dish represents a community, a tradition, and a way of life that persists despite rapid modernization.
